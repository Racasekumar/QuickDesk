# New Employee Onboarding

Last updated: 2026-06-15

New employees receive a company account, standard software access, and equipment based on role and location. Complete the onboarding checklist sent by HR and activate multi-factor authentication before accessing company systems. Your manager should confirm additional applications, shared drives, or groups needed for your work.

If an expected item is missing, submit one ticket per issue and include your start date, department, manager, and affected system. This helps support route the request correctly. Do not use another employee’s credentials while waiting. Support will update the ticket when setup is complete or approval is required.
