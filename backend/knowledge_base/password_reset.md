# Password Reset Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-10

Use the company self-service password portal to reset a forgotten password. Choose “Forgot password”, enter your company email, and complete identity verification. Create a new password that meets the displayed requirements. Do not reuse a recent password or share the new password with anyone, including support staff.

After a reset, allow a few minutes for the password to synchronize with email, VPN, and other services. Sign out of affected applications and sign in again. If your account becomes locked after repeated attempts, stop trying and submit a ticket with the application name and error shown. Support can unlock the account after verifying your identity.
