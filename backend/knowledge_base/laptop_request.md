# Laptop Request Process

Last updated: 2026-05-05

Request a company laptop through the helpdesk when joining the company, replacing damaged equipment, or needing an approved upgrade. Include your manager, office location, required date, and business reason. Standard devices are assigned based on role and availability; specialised hardware may need additional approval.

For damaged or failing equipment, describe the issue and include the asset tag if available. Do not open company hardware or install unsupported parts. Support may ask you to bring the device to the office or arrange collection. Back up approved work files to company storage before handing in a device.
